About FLUX ISA v3.0

FLUX (Fleet Language with Unified eXecution) is a register-based bytecode ISA for multi-agent fleet coordination. This sandbox implements the full reference VM in pure JavaScript — 16 GP registers, 16 FP registers, 16 VR registers, all in your browser.

Supported opcodes: Halt, Nop, Ret, Push, Pop, Dup, Swap, IMov, FMov, IAdd, ISub, IMul, IDiv, IMod, INeg, IAbs, IAnd, IOr, IXor, IShl, IShr, INot, ICmpEq, ICmpNe, ICmpLt, ICmpLe, ICmpGt, ICmpGe, IInc, IDec, Load8, Load32, Store8, Store32, LoadAddr, StackAlloc, FAdd, FSin, FCos, FSqrt, IToF, FToI, BToI, IToB, Jump, JumpIf, JumpIfNot, Call, and more.
